How to Play

Welcome to the Guess Who Game! Follow these steps to start playing:

  1. Select a topic from the home page.
  2. On the game page, a board with profile cards will appear.
  3. Click on a profile card to flip it over.
  4. Continue flipping until only one card remains unflipped.
  5. Use your deduction skills to identify the mystery profile.

If you have any questions, please feel free to contact our support team.
