How to Play & Ideas

I recommend that you start off by asking students who has played Guess Who, then introduce this mathematical version of the game.

There are currently 5 different topics, Number, Polyhedra, Graphs, Algebra & History of Maths. We aim to develop each of these into an easy/medium/hard version, which will then be able to be used with a variety of year groups. The prompt sheets available on the left are perfect to help scaffold some students to higher levels of thinking, or to give you rough script ideas while the students are working through a round.

The History of Maths topic will go together with a few other topics, focusing on pi, the golden ratio & fractals, to provide an indepth look at deeper topics in Mathematics. These topics are perfect for strong students who want to learn more, or to fill bonus lesson/form time.
